# Obsidian Note Capture Templates

## Atomic Note

```md
---
title: "<title>"
tags: [concept]
status: seed
created: "<YYYY-MM-DD>"
---

# <title>

## Summary

<short statement of the idea>

## Details

<supporting explanation>

## Related Notes

- [[Related Note]]
```

## Meeting Note

```md
---
title: "<meeting title>"
tags: [meeting]
participants: [<name-1>, <name-2>]
created: "<YYYY-MM-DD>"
status: draft
---

# <meeting title>

## Context

<why this meeting happened>

## Notes

- note
- note

## Decisions

- decision

## Action Items

- [ ] follow up item

## Related Notes

- [[Project Note]]
```

## Fleeting Idea

```md
---
title: "<idea title>"
tags: [idea]
status: seed
created: "<YYYY-MM-DD>"
---

# <idea title>

## Idea

<raw idea converted into clear prose>

## Why It Matters

<expected value or implication>

## Next Step

- [ ] validate or expand this idea
```
